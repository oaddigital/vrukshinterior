Who We Are

Crafting Soulful Spaces

At Vruksh Interior, we believe that every home has a spirit. Like a tree ('Vruksh') that stands tall and provides shelter, our furniture is designed to be the sturdy, beautiful foundation of your daily life.

We specialize in blending the durability of premium Plywood with the modern aesthetics and resilience of PVC. Whether it's a modular kitchen that withstands the heat or a wardrobe that defines your bedroom, we craft it with precision.
